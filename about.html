My Story

Hello! I'm Shivam Sahu, a passionate interior designer with a Master's degree in Interior and Retail Design from the prestigious World University of Design. My journey in design began with a deep fascination for how spaces can influence human emotions and behavior.

At Shivam Interior, I believe that every space has a story to tell. My approach combines contemporary design principles with sustainable practices, creating environments that are not only beautiful but also mindful of our planet's resources.

With expertise in both residential and commercial design, I specialize in creating spaces that reflect the unique personality and needs of each client while maintaining the highest standards of functionality and aesthetics.

My Philosophy

I believe in designing with purpose - every element, every material, and every color choice serves a specific function while contributing to the overall harmony of the space. My designs are characterized by:

  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient solutions
  • Functionality: Spaces that work seamlessly for their intended purpose
  • Timelessness: Designs that remain relevant and beautiful for years to come
  • Personalization: Each project reflects the unique vision of its occupants
